Top 5 Techniques to Fall Asleep Faster

Falling asleep quickly can improve your quality of life. Here are five scientifically-backed tips:

1. Practice deep breathing (4-7-8 technique)
2. Block blue light before bed using screen filters
3. Keep a consistent sleep schedule
4. Avoid caffeine after 3PM
5. Try magnesium or chamomile tea as natural relaxers

These methods work best when combined with a relaxing bedtime routine.
